Who Are WE?

The Radiation Group Inc. was established in 2003 and is based in Orange County, California. Our principal, Munir Shoga has worked in the radiation effects industry for over 30 years supporting major aerospace companies and their contractors. He has a Ph. D. degree in electrical engineering, holds four US patents, and has extensive experience in radiation effects on space and ground systems.

A group of scientists and engineers with wide range of expertise are available, if needed.

Radiation Group Inc. provides radiation effects analysis and test services for space, avionics, and ground systems. :

Our Experience

  1. Radiation assessment of ~75 space craft program.
  2. Over 2000 hours of beam time test experience in single event effects, prompt dose, total ionizing dose, and displacement damage testing.
  3. Tested complex parts include FPGAs, Processors, memory devices (DDR1, DDR2, DDR3, Flash NAND, SRAM, Quad Data Rate(QDR), Synchronous SDRAM, etc.), Digital signal Processor(DSP), PLLs, etc.,
  4. Performed TID, displacement damage, prompt dose testing and analysis on many part types.
